American Sardine Ale

Originally brewed exclusively for our friends at American Sardine Bar in South Philadelphia, this golden Belgian ale has quickly become one of our top-selling brands.

ABV: 7.0%
COLOR: 8 SRM
IBU: 70
MALTS: Premium Two-Row
HOPS: 4 lbs
BBL: Bittering— Magnum; Late Kettle—Cascade, Centennial; Dry Hops—Amarillo, Cascade, Centennial, Citra, Chinook, Simcoe
FERMENTATION: 100% Stainless Steel Fermentation
COMMENTS

Simple and highly approachable, ASA offers complex aromas of lemon zest and fresh-cracked black pepper.
